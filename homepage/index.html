KJ Somaiya College of Engineering (KJSCE) is a reputed engineering institution located in Mumbai and a constituent college of Somaiya Vidyavihar University, known for its strong academic foundation, innovation-driven environment, and industry-oriented education. The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs across key engineering disciplines such as Computer Engineering, Information Technology, Electronics and Telecommunication, Mechanical Engineering, and Artificial Intelligence & Data Science. With modern infrastructure, experienced faculty, active student clubs, research opportunities, and strong industry connections, KJSCE provides students with a holistic learning experience and prepares them for successful careers in technology and engineering.The college offers high-quality undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various engineering disciplines, focusing on technical expertise, research, and industry-oriented learning.
